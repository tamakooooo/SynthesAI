## 📋 Core Brand Identity

### Name
**SynthesAI**

### Pronunciation
`/sɪnθ-sai/` (sin-th-sai)

### Meaning
- **Synthesize** (综合/整合) - Bringing together multiple sources into unified knowledge
- **AI** - Artificial Intelligence driving the synthesis process

### Concept
SynthesAI represents the synthesis of:
- 📹 **Video content** → Structured summaries
- 📝 **Web articles** → Knowledge cards
- 📚 **Vocabulary** → Learning materials
